RC1005 Water Traversal

You arrive for your Water Traversal class, where your professor is just getting started for the day. "As a reminder, our practical exam will be next week," she mentions, "so don't forget to register your choice of activity to be tested on. You can choose to jetski or parasail across the Straits of Johor." That sounds dangerous! You decide to see if you can sign up with a somewhat less life-threatening activity.

What safer alternative might you be able to propose?

There's many ways to go out at sea, but it's best to follow the directions you have.
