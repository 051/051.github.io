Solution: RC1003 Fortune Telling

Answer: CONTEXT MENU

The 11 pictures in this puzzle are cropped from 11 signs around the Leo floors. As hinted by the mention of "what comes next" in the flavor text, we can find each of the signs and extract the letter following the letters in the picture. Taking these 11 letters and putting them in the order of the signs in the puzzle spells out the answer CONTEXT MENU.
