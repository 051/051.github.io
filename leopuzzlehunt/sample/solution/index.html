Solution: Double or Nothing

Answer: MIRROR IMAGES

Looking over each of the crossword clues, we might notice that each clue appears to have some letters missing. Some observation might reveal each clue has exactly one double letter that has been completely removed from the clue, as hinted at by the title "Double or Nothing".

In fact, upon solving each of the clues, as in the table below, solvers might notice that none of the answers appear to be present in the wordsearch grid provided. In fact, each answer also contains a double letter that has to be removed in order to find the word in the grid.

Clue Answer
Matters of importance ISSUES
Round objects that vehicles roll on WHEELS
Sewing tool NEEDLE
Wooden keg often used to store wine BARREL
Drowsy; in need of a nap SLEEPY
Directions in a crossword: Down and ____ ACROSS
Meat often used in curry MUTTON
Opposite of rough SMOOTH
Long, narrow box bodies are buried in COFFIN
Continuously throw and catch items (often balls) JUGGLE
____ blossom (sakura) CHERRY
US state with a pizza named after it HAWAII
Surface at the back of a boat used to steer it RUDDER

The flavor text tells us to "take stock of our losses". Looking at the pairs of letters we removed from the answers, we might notice they spell out SEE REST OF GRID. Taking the letters from the grid that weren't used as part of any word spells out the message THE ANSWER IS MIOR IMAGES. There's another double-letter pair missing from this message, so the answer is MIRROR IMAGES.
