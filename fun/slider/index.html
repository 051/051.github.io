Slider puzzle

Click a tile to try to move it. You can move multiple tiles in the same row or column at once (click the furthest tile and it will push other tiles). Complete the puzzle by sorting all the tiles into the correct order (1-24 in reading order, with the hole in the bottom-right).

Hotkeys: press [space] for "Start" or "Finish"; press [r] for "Restart".

Note: press "Finish" / [space] to mark a game as complete (your timer will keep running until you do).
