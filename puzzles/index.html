Hunt puzzles

I write hunt puzzles sometimes! Here are some of my favourite puzzles I've written; each of them has a final answer which is a single English word or phrase.

  • Dimmed Wits (PDF) (SPH 2020): a puzzle about hypothetical puzzlers in an alternate universe.
  • White as a Sheet (Silph 2021): a puzzle that seems like it gives you nothing to work with.
  • Six Feet Under (Silph 2021): a labyrinthine collection of footnotes.
  • The Minimeta that Goes Wrong (Silph 2021): a chaotic minimeta where the constructor can't seem to catch a break.
  • Re-actions (Silph 2021): a tough-as-nails cryptic crossword where you'll have to figure out how the clues fit together.

Cryptic crosswords

These are cryptic crosswords with no hunt-style "final answer", though usually they have instructions for extracting one or more phrases from the grid.

  • Choose Your Path (PDF), written around the time of Undertale's release on the Switch. (Spoiler warning!)
  • The Two-Trench Truce (PDF), written around the 100th anniversary of the end of World War I.
  • Hexed Again! (PDF), written as part of a Secret Santa puzzle exchange for Christmas 2019.
